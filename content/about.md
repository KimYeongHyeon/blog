## Biography

I am a Ph.D student at BiLab, Seoul National University, working on biomedical AI, medical image analysis, and agentic AI systems for clinical research. My work focuses on CT artifact reduction, few-shot learning, and multi-agent frameworks that connect clinical questions with reproducible real-world evidence.
